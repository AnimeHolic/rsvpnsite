Privacy Policy

PDFUnlocker Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 28, 2025

1. Introduction

PDFUnlocker is a mobile application designed to remove passwords from PDF files. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Data We Collect

PDFUnlocker does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (e.g., name, email, or device identifiers) unless explicitly required for the app's functionality. The app only accesses the following data:

  • PDF Files: The app accesses PDF files selected by you to remove passwords and save the unlocked versions.
  • No Other Data: We do not collect location data, contacts, or any other personal information.

3. Permissions

PDFUnlocker requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Storage Permission (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE and WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, Android SDK ≤ 33): Allows the app to read the selected PDF file and save the unlocked PDF.
  • Manage External Storage (MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, Android SDK > 29): Required for file access in modern Android versions.

These permissions are solely used to enable the core functionality of selecting, processing, and saving PDF files.

4. How We Use Your Data

The data accessed by PDFUnlocker is processed locally on your device to remove passwords. We do not upload or transmit your PDF files.

5. Data Security

All PDF processing is done locally. The app does not connect to the internet for file processing, ensuring full data privacy.

6. Third-Party Libraries

PDFUnlocker uses the following libraries:

  • Syncfusion Flutter PDF: For PDF processing
  • File Picker: For file selection
  • Permission Handler: For permissions
  • Device Info Plus: For Android compatibility

These libraries may collect anonymous usage data but no personal data.

7. Your Rights

We do not collect personal information. You have full control over your files and can delete them anytime.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy as needed. The updated version will be posted with a new date.
